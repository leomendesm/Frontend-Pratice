About Sana

Sana Minatozaki (Japanese: 湊崎 紗夏 Hepburn: Minatozaki Sana), known mononymously as Sana (Hangul: 사나), was born on December 29, 1996 (age 20) in Tennōji-ku, Osaka, Japan. Sana attended EXPG (Exile Professional Gym) Osaka branch, a dance and vocal studio, from 2009 to early 2012. In her last year of middle school, she was scouted by JYP Entertainment on the street and auditioned on April 13, 2012.
